About Us

Edufun Technik delivers world-class immersive STEM education to kids and works to get more girls and young people into STEM careers.

Our Story

Founded in 2014, we teach kids Coding, Robotics, Web & Mobile App Development and other in-demand STEM Skills. We run in-school, afterschool, weekend, holiday and online Tech Boot Camps. We are raising tomorrow's thinkers, innovators, and creators.

We are firm believers in cultivating the hidden potential that all children possess. Our professionally designed programs enrich the school curriculum by awakening your children’s undiscovered skills and nurturing them for future success based on their natural abilities and interests.

Meet Our Founder/CEO

Uchenna has and over seven (7) years’ experience in STEM Curriculum Development and Implementation of Gender-Enhancing STEM Education for Children/youths in a resource-limited/under-privileged communities in Nigeria.

Currently, Edufun Technik is providing 15000 Pupils, and 17800 Students and Youths termly with practical hands-on training with the platform to acquire the 21st century skill and gives exposure to Computer Science, Robotics and Artificial Intelligence. skills and nurturing them for future success based on their natural abilities and interests.
